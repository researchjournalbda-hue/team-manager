To share this across the team and merge with Google Calendar, deploy the companion Code.gs and paste its /exec URL into CONFIG.scriptUrl: Save writes the roster and assignments to your Sheet, Load roster from Sheet pulls the four columns back in, and Push creates a calendar invite for every assignment.
